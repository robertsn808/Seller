Setup Instructions
How to Configure Your Email for Professional Sending

Set these environment variables on your server or in your .env file:

EMAIL_SMTP_HOST=smtp.yourmailprovider.com
EMAIL_SMTP_PORT=587
EMAIL_USERNAME=your-email@yourdomain.com
EMAIL_PASSWORD=your-app-password
EMAIL_FROM_DOMAIN=yourdomain.com
EMAIL_REPLY_TO=support@yourdomain.com
Note: Use app-specific passwords for Gmail, Outlook, etc. Never use your main account password.

Your DKIM record is already configured! Set these environment variables:

DKIM_ENABLED=true
DKIM_DOMAIN=yourdomain.com
DKIM_SELECTOR=default
Your DKIM is Ready! The DNS record you provided is properly formatted and will authenticate your emails.
